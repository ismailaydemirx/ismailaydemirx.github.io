I'm Ismail Aydemir, a final-year Software Engineering student at Karadeniz Technical University. Through the Erasmus program, I had the opportunity to study at Universidad de Zaragoza, which helped me improve my communication skills and gain experience in working within an international culture.

My goal is to build effective and sustainable software solutions for real-world problems.

I'm always open to new projects, creative ideas, and collaborating with dynamic teams.
